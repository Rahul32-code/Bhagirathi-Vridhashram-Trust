Support with Compassion: Uplifting Lives of the Elderly

At Bhagirathi Vridhashram Trust, we believe that every elderly person deserves care, dignity, and love in their golden years. Your kind donations help us provide shelter, nutritious meals, medical assistance, and emotional support to those who have no one else to turn to.
