ABOUT Bhagirathi Vridhashram Trust

Together, We Create a Home Filled with Care and Compassion

At Bhagirathi Ashram Trust, we believe that every individual deserves to live with dignity, love, and respect. Our ashram is more than just a place to stay—it’s a true home, where hearts unite, stories come to life, and lives are nurtured with care and compassion. Here, we foster a community where every individual is valued and cherished.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every elder is treated with the dignity, respect, and care they deserve. We are committed to providing comprehensive support—physical, emotional, and spiritual—so that each resident can experience a fulfilling and meaningful life.

A Home Filled with Care

Our A small act of kindness can create a world of difference. For just ₹4,000 (approx. $50), you can provide comfort, warmth, and nutritious meals to a resident for an entire week. Your generous support ensures they stay healthy, happy, and full of life in a safe, loving home where care knows no bounds.

Care Tailored to You

Your generosity enables our devoted medical and nursing team to provide compassionate, personalized care 24/7. From regular health checkups to emotional support, we ensure each resident lives with comfort, dignity, and the security of knowing they are truly cared for—just like family.

Creating Cherished Memories

Your generous support allows us to host joyful events, festive celebrations, and meaningful activities that brighten our residents' lives with light and companionship. Each smile shared, each laugh heard, is a cherished memory made possible by your kindness.

Healthcare with Dignity

Your generosity ensures that each resident receives round-the-clock healthcare, coupled with the respect, compassion, and comfort they deserve. Whether it’s managing chronic conditions or providing emotional support, we deliver care that not only heals but also preserves the dignity of every individual. Because here, healthcare is about treating people, not just patients.
